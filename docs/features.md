# Application Features

Personal Guru is packed with features designed to create a comprehensive and interactive learning experience.

## Core Learning Experience

- **User Accounts**: Secure sign-up, login, and profile management to keep your progress safe.
- **Topic Isolation**: Each user has their own private learning materials and topics, ensuring a personalized workspace.
- **Dynamic Study Plans**: Enter *any* topic and receive a custom, step-by-step study plan generated specifically for you.
- **Detailed Study Content**: Each step in your personalized study plan includes comprehensive, AI-generated reading material.
- **Interactive Learning**: Progress through the plan one step at a time, ensuring mastery before moving on.
- **Personalized Background**: Set your own user background (e.g., "I am a beginner", "I am a senior developer") to tailor the complexity of the learning content to your current level.

## Multi-Modal Learning

- **Text-to-Speech (TTS)**: Listen to each learning step with integrated, high-quality audio.
- **Voice Input (STT)**: Use your microphone to interact with the AI assistant and navigate the application hands-free.
- **Podcast Generation**: Transform any topic into a dialogue-based audio podcast for on-the-go learning.
- **Reel Mode**: A TikTok/Reel-style interface for browsing short, educational videos related to your topic.

## Assessment & Practice

- **Flashcards**: Review vocabulary and key concepts with interactive flashcards generated for your specific topics.
- **Knowledge Assessment**: Answer multiple-choice questions after each step to test your understanding.
- **Adaptive Learning**: The study plan adapts based on your performance in the "Check Your Understanding" quizzes.
- **Instant Feedback**: Receive immediate feedback on your answers to reinforce learning.
- **Code Sandbox**: Execute Python code securely within the application for interactive programming exercises.

## Productivity & Tools

- **Q&A Chat**: Ask questions about the study material and get instant answers from an AI assistant context-aware of your current lesson.
- **Export to Markdown**: At the end of a course, export the entire study plan and content to a Markdown file. Perfect for importing into note-taking apps like Notion, Obsidian, or NotebookLM.
- **Background Database Viewer**: A built-in admin tool to view, sort, and manage database records (includes bulk delete functionality).

## Technical Features

- **Local AI Integration**: Designed to connect with locally-hosted AI services (LLM, TTS) such as Ollama and Kokoro for privacy and control.
- **Comprehensive Test Suite**: Includes a full suite of unit tests to verify application logic and ensure stability.
